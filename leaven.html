A sourdough platform

Maintain sourdough cultures through a simple and fluid design, made with bakers in mind. No more spreadsheets or calculators required when baking sourdough.

Knows your starter

Manage multiple cultures without needing to memorise individual culture traits. Leaven keeps track of details like: flour and water weights; container weights; typical activation time; and starter hydration.

It's free

Leaven is a free app, but I will accept donations through the form of baked goods ;-)
